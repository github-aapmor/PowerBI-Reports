# Adidas sales Report

# About data : 

The data includes information on sales, revenue, and profit for Adidas products in various areas and cities within the United Kingdom (UK) and the United States (USA). The data encompasses stores, retailers, and products, and covers the years 2020 and 2021

# Source of Data

We collected the dataset from the Kaggle platform.

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/heemalichaudhari/adidas-sales-dataset
